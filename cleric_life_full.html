The Shepherd  ·  Cleric · Life  ·  Abilities
Class Abilities
Healing Word Bonus Action · costs 1 spell · range 60ft
One creature you can see regains 2D4 + WIS hit points. You can do this and still take your full action this turn.
Cure Wounds Action · costs 1 spell · touch
Touch a creature. It regains 4D8 + WIS hit points. The bigger heal — use when someone is critically low and you can reach them safely.
Bless Action · costs 1 spell · concentration
Choose up to 3 willing creatures within 30ft. For 1 minute, they each add 1D4 to every attack roll and saving throw. One of the best spells in combat — cast it early.
Spiritual Weapon Action · costs 1 spell · lasts 1 minute
A spectral mace appears within 60ft. For the next minute, use your bonus action each turn to move it 20ft and attack one creature: D20 + WIS vs AC, 1D8 + WIS radiant damage on a hit. Does not require concentration.
Guardian of Faith Action · costs 1 spell
A towering spectral guardian appears in a space within 30ft. Any hostile creature that enters or starts its turn within 10ft of it must make a DEX save (DC 14) or take 20 radiant damage, half on success. The guardian vanishes after dealing 60 total damage.
Preserve Life Action · Channel Divinity · range 30ft
Distribute 25 hit points among any number of creatures within 30ft. No creature can receive more than half its maximum HP from this. Cross off a square on page 1.
Weapons
Mace Action · melee 5ft
Roll D20 + STR vs AC to hit. On a hit, deal 1D6 + STR bludgeoning damage.
General Combat Actions
Dash Action
Gain an extra 30ft of movement this turn.
Dodge Action
Until your next turn, attacks against you have disadvantage and you have advantage on DEX saves.
Disengage Action
Your movement does not provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of this turn.
Opportunity Attack Reaction · when an enemy leaves your reach
Make one mace attack: D20 + STR vs AC, 1D6 + STR on a hit. Triggered when a creature moves out of your reach without Disengaging.
Help Action
Give an ally advantage on their next ability check (within 5ft), or on their next attack against a creature within 5ft of you.
Hide Action
Roll DEX (Stealth). If you succeed and are out of direct sight, you become hidden. Attacks against hidden targets are at disadvantage.

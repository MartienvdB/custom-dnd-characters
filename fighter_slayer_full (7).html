The Thornwhisper  ·  Warlock · Archfey  ·  Abilities
Class Abilities
Eldritch Blast Action · at will
Hurl a beam of fey starlight at one creature within 120ft. Roll D20 + CHA vs AC to hit. On a hit, deal 2D10 force damage. The target makes a WIS save (DC 14) or is frightened of you until the end of their next turn.
Silvery Barbs Reaction · costs 1 spell
When a creature you can see within 60ft succeeds on a d20 roll, use your reaction to spend 1 spell and force them to reroll, taking the lower result. Use this to turn a hit into a miss, or a passed save into a failed one.
Hypnotic Pattern Action · costs 1 spell · concentration
A swirling pattern of fey colours fills a 30ft cube within 120ft. Every creature in the area makes a WIS save (DC 14) or becomes incapacitated with speed 0 for 1 minute. The effect ends for a creature that takes damage or is shaken free by an ally's action.
Hold Person Action · costs 1 spell · concentration
Point at one humanoid within 60ft. They make a WIS save (DC 14) or are paralysed for 1 minute. A paralysed creature automatically fails STR and DEX saves, and all attacks against them have advantage. They repeat the save at the end of each of their turns.
Misty Step Bonus Action · costs 1 spell
Vanish in a shimmer of silver mist and reappear in any unoccupied space you can see within 30ft. You can use this even when surrounded.
Vespertine's Errand Bonus Action
Direct Vespertine to move up to 40ft and do one of the following: harry a creature — it has disadvantage on its next attack roll; retrieve or drop a small object; or scout an adjacent room and report back. If Vespertine is destroyed, resummon them after a long rest.
Weapons
Dagger Action · melee 5ft or thrown 20/60ft
Roll D20 + DEX vs AC to hit. On a hit, deal 1D4 + DEX piercing damage. Thrown beyond 20ft is at disadvantage.
General Combat Actions
Dash Action
Gain an extra 30ft of movement this turn.
Dodge Action
Until your next turn, attacks against you have disadvantage and you have advantage on DEX saves.
Disengage Action
Your movement does not provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of this turn.
Opportunity Attack Reaction · when an enemy leaves your reach
Make one dagger attack: D20 + DEX vs AC, 1D4 + DEX on a hit. Triggered when a creature moves out of your reach without Disengaging.
Help Action
Give an ally advantage on their next ability check (within 5ft), or on their next attack against a creature within 5ft of you.
Hide Action
Roll DEX (Stealth). If you succeed and are out of direct sight, you become hidden. Attacks against hidden targets are at disadvantage.

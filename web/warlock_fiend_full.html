The Blightcaller  ·  Warlock · The Fiend  ·  Abilities
Class Abilities
Eldritch Blast Action · at will
Hurl a bolt of hellfire at one creature within 120ft. Roll D20 + CHA vs AC to hit. On a hit, deal 2D10 force damage and push the target 10ft away (Tome of Blasting).
Hellish Rebuke Reaction · costs 1 spell · when you are damaged
When a creature damages you, react to punish it. It makes a DEX save (DC 14) or takes 4D10 fire damage, half on success.
Hunger of Hadar Action · costs 1 spell · concentration
Open a void at a point within 150ft. A 20ft sphere of impenetrable blackness forms for up to 1 minute. Creatures inside are blinded and take 2D6 cold damage at the start of their turns.
Fireball Action · costs 1 spell
A bead of hellfire explodes at a point within 150ft. Every creature in a 20ft radius makes a DEX save (DC 14) or takes 8D6 fire damage, half on success.
Banishment Action · costs 1 spell · concentration
One creature within 60ft makes a CHA save (DC 14). On a failure it is removed from the fight. It returns at the start of your next turn unless you concentrate for 1 minute — removing it permanently.
Dark One's Blessing Bonus Action · on kill
When you drop a creature to 0 HP, gain 8 temporary hit points. These absorb damage before your real HP. Cannot stack.
General Combat Actions
Dash Action
Gain an extra 30ft of movement this turn.
Dodge Action
Until your next turn, attacks against you have disadvantage and you have advantage on DEX saves.
Disengage Action
Your movement does not provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of this turn.
Opportunity Attack Reaction · when an enemy leaves your reach
Make one dagger attack: D20 + DEX vs AC, 1D4 + DEX on a hit. Triggered when a creature moves out of melee range without using Disengage.
Help Action
Give an ally advantage on their next ability check (must be within 5ft), or on their next attack against a creature within 5ft of you.
Hide Action
Roll DEX (Stealth). If you succeed and are out of direct sight, you become hidden. Attacks against hidden targets are at disadvantage.

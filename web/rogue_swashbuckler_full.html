The Duelist  ·  Rogue · Swashbuckler  ·  Abilities
Class Abilities
Sneak Attack Passive · once per turn
Once per turn, when you hit with a weapon attack, add 3D6 damage if any of the following are true: you have advantage on the roll; an ally is within 5ft of the target; or no other creature is within 5ft of you (Rakish Audacity — you fight sharpest alone).
Fancy Footwork Passive · on melee hit
When you hit a creature with a melee attack, that creature cannot make opportunity attacks against you for the rest of this turn. Step in, strike, and step out safely.
Panache Action · 1 use / short rest
Target one creature within 30ft that can hear you. It makes a WIS save (DC 11). On a failure, choose one: it is charmed by you for 1 minute and will not attack you unless attacked first; or it is taunted and can only target you with attacks until the end of your next turn. Cross off the resource dot on page 1.
Cunning Action Bonus Action
Use your bonus action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide instead of your action. Strike and vanish, or reposition without burning your turn.
Uncanny Dodge Reaction
When an attacker you can see hits you, use your reaction to halve the damage from that hit.
General Combat Actions
Dash Action
Gain an extra 30ft of movement this turn.
Dodge Action
Until your next turn, attacks against you have disadvantage and you have advantage on DEX saves.
Disengage Action
Your movement does not provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of this turn.
Opportunity Attack Reaction · when an enemy leaves your reach
Make one rapier attack: D20 + DEX vs AC, 1D8 + DEX on a hit. Triggered when a creature moves out of your reach without Disengaging.
Help Action
Give an ally advantage on their next ability check (within 5ft), or on their next attack against a creature within 5ft of you.
Hide Action
Roll DEX (Stealth). If you succeed and are out of direct sight, you become hidden. Attacks against hidden targets are at disadvantage — and trigger Sneak Attack.

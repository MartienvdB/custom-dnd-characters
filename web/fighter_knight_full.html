The Bulwark  ·  Fighter · Knight  ·  Abilities
Class Abilities
Attack ×2 Action
When you take the Attack action, you can make two weapon attacks instead of one.
Pushing Attack Action · unlimited
Make a longsword attack as normal. On a hit, in addition to the damage, push the target up to 15ft in any direction. No save — they move.
Menacing Attack Action · unlimited
Make a longsword attack as normal. On a hit, the target must make a WIS save (DC 13). On a failure, it is frightened of you until the end of its next turn. While frightened, it has disadvantage on all attack rolls and cannot willingly move closer to you.
Action Surge No action cost · 1 use / short rest
On your turn, take one additional full Action. Use this to attack four times in a single turn, or combine an attack with another action. Cross off the resource dot on page 1.
Second Wind Bonus Action · 1 use / short rest
Catch your breath mid-combat. Regain 1D10 + 3 hit points. Cross off the resource dot on page 1.
Shield Wall Reaction · unlimited
When a creature within 5ft of you attacks an ally, you may use your reaction to interpose your shield. The attacker has disadvantage on that attack roll.
General Combat Actions
Dash Action
Gain an extra 30ft of movement this turn.
Dodge Action
Until your next turn, attacks against you have disadvantage and you have advantage on DEX saves.
Disengage Action
Your movement does not provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of this turn.
Opportunity Attack Reaction · when an enemy leaves your reach
Make one longsword attack: D20 + STR vs AC, 1D8 + STR on a hit. Triggered when a creature moves out of your reach without Disengaging.
Help Action
Give an ally advantage on their next ability check (within 5ft), or on their next attack against a creature within 5ft of you.
Hide Action
Roll DEX (Stealth). If you succeed and are out of direct sight, you become hidden. Attacks against hidden targets are at disadvantage.

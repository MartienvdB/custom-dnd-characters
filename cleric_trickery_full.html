The Veil  ·  Cleric · Trickery  ·  Abilities
Class Abilities
Healing Word Bonus Action · costs 1 spell · range 60ft
One creature you can see regains 2D4 + WIS hit points. You can still take your full action this turn. This isn't your focus — save it for when an ally is about to go down.
Charm Person Action · costs 1 spell · range 30ft
One humanoid makes a WIS save (DC 14) or regards you as a friendly acquaintance for 1 hour. They will not attack you and are disposed to help you. They know they were charmed once the effect ends.
Hold Person Action · costs 1 spell · concentration · range 60ft
One humanoid makes a WIS save (DC 14) or is paralysed for 1 minute. Paralysed creatures automatically fail STR and DEX saves, and all attacks against them have advantage. They repeat the save at the end of each of their turns.
Hypnotic Pattern Action · costs 1 spell · concentration · range 120ft
A twisting pattern fills a 30ft cube. Every creature in the area makes a WIS save (DC 14) or becomes incapacitated with speed 0 for 1 minute. The effect ends for a creature that takes damage or is shaken free by an ally's action.
Invoke Duplicity Action · Channel Divinity · lasts 1 minute
Summon a perfect illusory copy of yourself in an unoccupied space within 30ft. While it persists, attackers cannot tell you apart — attack rolls against you have disadvantage. Use your bonus action each turn to move the duplicate up to 30ft. Cross off a square on page 1.
Move Duplicate Bonus Action · while Invoke Duplicity is active
Move your illusory duplicate up to 30ft to any space you can see. Reposition it to keep attackers guessing or to flank alongside allies.
Weapons
Dagger Action · melee 5ft or thrown 20/60ft
Roll D20 + DEX vs AC to hit. On a hit, deal 1D4 + DEX piercing damage. Thrown beyond 20ft is at disadvantage.
Shortbow Action · range 80/320ft
Roll D20 + DEX vs AC to hit. On a hit, deal 1D6 + DEX piercing damage. Attacks beyond 80ft are at disadvantage.
General Combat Actions
Dash Action
Gain an extra 30ft of movement this turn.
Dodge Action
Until your next turn, attacks against you have disadvantage and you have advantage on DEX saves.
Disengage Action
Your movement does not provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of this turn.
Opportunity Attack Reaction · when an enemy leaves your reach
Make one dagger attack: D20 + DEX vs AC, 1D4 + DEX on a hit. Triggered when a creature moves out of your reach without Disengaging.
Help Action
Give an ally advantage on their next ability check (within 5ft), or on their next attack against a creature within 5ft of you.
Hide Action
Roll DEX (Stealth). If you succeed and are out of direct sight, you become hidden. Attacks against hidden targets are at disadvantage.
